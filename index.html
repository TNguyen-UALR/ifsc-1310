Thoughts on Accessibility

Web Accessibility is an important and likely underimplemented area in most websites. The idea of web accessibility is to allow people with disability to access websites and pages on the internet without much issue. For example, the visually impaired may need a page magnifier, reader, or speech activated assistant so they are not forced to have someone else beside them at all times. I imagine it can be difficult to think that because of a disability, some if not most activites, entertainment, and information are now out of your reach. Instead of being stuck with that idea, a little more thought and effort could be put into audience accessibility so no one feels like their disability keeps them from what they are looking for.
